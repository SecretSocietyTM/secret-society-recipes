Super Simple Tuna Mayo

Description:

This is a simple yet delicious dish packing a lot of protein that can be made in minutes. No prep time, almost no cooking time and can be topped and served with anything youd like.

I usually prefer mine with roasted seaweed sheets for a bit of umame flavor server over warm rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 cans Tuna, whichever you prefer
  • 105g Light/Low Fat Mayonaisse
  • 15mL Light Soy Sauce
  • 6g Furikake Rice Seasoning

Steps

  1. Drain the canned tuna and place in a bowl. Depending on the type of tuna you use you will have to smash the tuna into smaller chunks with a fork unless you like bigger pieces
  2. Add the mayo, soy sauce and furikake and stir. Continue to stir until the darkness from the soy sauce has spread out evenely accross all the tuna
  3. And you are done, simply serve with whatever else you like and enjoy
